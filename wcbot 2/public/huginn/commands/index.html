Commands & Alerts

Everything you can send to Huginn, and everything it sends back, automatically.

Commands you send

/follow [team]

Start receiving live alerts for every match featuring that team. Works in any WhatsApp group or direct chat.

/unfollow [team]

Stop receiving alerts for a specific team. Your other followed teams are not affected.

/live

Show all currently active World Cup matches, live scores, and current odds in a clean summary.

/schedule

View the full list of upcoming fixtures and kickoff times for the remainder of the tournament.

/stats [player]

Get a full player profile containing career goals, assists, playing style, known injury history, and what to expect from them at this World Cup. Powered by AI.

/status

See which teams you are currently following and which commentary style is active.

/style hype

Switch to high-energy, dramatic commentary. Every goal feels like a final.

/style tactical

Switch to calm, data-driven analysis. xG, formations, and odds movements explained clearly.

/style funny

Switch to light-hearted banter mode. Football is entertainment after all.

/style balanced

The default. Friendly, informative, and never annoying.

/style

Check the current commentary style configuration for your chat.

/help

Lost or need a quick refresher? Send this command straight to the chat at any time.
