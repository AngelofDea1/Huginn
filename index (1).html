How it works

Three steps.
Then just watch.

No app store. No account. No configuration. Add the bot once and it runs for every match you follow, automatically.

01 — ADD
💬
Add to your group

Add MatchPulse to any WhatsApp group the same way you'd add a contact. Works in existing groups with your friends and family.

02 — FOLLOW
🎯
Type /follow

Send /follow Nigeria and the bot locks onto that match. You can follow multiple matches at once.

03 — WATCH
⚡
Get every moment

Pre-match bulletin, goals, red cards, half-time report, odds shifts, and a full-time wrap — all delivered automatically, in real time.
